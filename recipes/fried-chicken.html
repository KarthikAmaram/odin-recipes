Fried Chicken

Description

This recipe will give you the best fried chicken you've ever tasted. It will take a bit over an hour and will have up to 8 servings.

It will be crispy and absolutely delicious!

Ingredients

  • 1 (4 pound) chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour for coating
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 quarts vegetable oil for frying
